MATLAB scripts for analyzing and breaking the UTS-CAPTCHA scheme proposed in the following paper. Note that the name UTS-CAPTCHA was not used by the authors of the following paper, but was proposed by us (Carlos Javier Hernandez-Castro, Shujun Li. Maria D. R-Moreno and David F. Barrero) in our paper.

Shinil Kwon and Sungdeok Cha. 2016. A Paradigm Shift for the CAPTCHA Race: Adding Uncertainty to the Process. IEEE Software 33, 6 (Nov 2016), 80-85. DOI:10.1109/MS.2016.32
